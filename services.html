Service 01

Schematic Review

A schematic review goes beyond what ERC catches. We validate electrical correctness, protection strategy, decoupling, sequencing, and datasheet compliance — before a single trace is routed.

Fixing a schematic error before layout is fast. Fixing the same error after fabrication means a respin.

  • Power supply design and sequencing
  • Decoupling strategy and capacitor selection
  • Reset circuitry and startup behaviour
  • ESD, reverse-polarity, and overcurrent protection
  • I²C, SPI, UART, USB, CAN interface validation
  • Crystal and clock network review
  • Footprint/symbol vs datasheet cross-check
  • MCU/FPGA pinout validation

Service 02

PCB Layout Review

DRC tells you if your layout is manufacturable. It says nothing about whether it will work. Signal integrity, EMI performance, and thermal behaviour are all determined by layout decisions DRC can't see.

We apply engineering judgment to your board — informed by years of debugging production failures and regulatory testing.

  • Return current path and ground plane analysis
  • High-speed signal routing and impedance control
  • Decoupling capacitor placement (within 1mm)
  • EMI: clock guards, filter placement, shielding gaps
  • Thermal: pads, via arrays, copper pour sizing
  • DFM: clearances, paste apertures, IPC compliance
  • Stack-up suitability for target frequencies
  • RF layout and antenna matching (if applicable)

Service 03

BOM Review

A design that is electrically perfect can still fail in production. An EOL component, a 20-week lead time on a critical IC, or a $1.50 part with a $0.08 equivalent — all preventable with a proper BOM review.

  • Lifecycle and EOL risk assessment
  • Lead time check across major distributors
  • Second-source and pin-compatible alternatives
  • Single-source dependency identification
  • Cost reduction opportunities
  • RoHS / REACH compliance check
  • Packaging and reel-size consolidation
