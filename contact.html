Before you send
Do I need to share files before getting a quote?+
No. You can get an indicative quote based on a brief description: layer count, board size, design type, and review scope. We'll confirm the final quote once we've seen your files.
How do I share files securely?+
After your initial enquiry we'll provide a secure transfer link. If you require an NDA before sharing, we can arrange that first — just mention it in your message.
Can I request rush turnaround?+
Yes. Rush turnaround (next business day) is available for appropriately sized designs. Mention your deadline in the form and we'll confirm whether it's feasible.
What information helps you give an accurate quote?+
The most useful information is: what the board does, layer count, approximate dimensions, which type of review you need, and any specific concerns or areas to focus on. Component count and design complexity also affect scope.
