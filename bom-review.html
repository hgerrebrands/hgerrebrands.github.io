BOM Review
Catch sourcing and production risks early

A structured review of your Bill of Materials to identify availability issues, incorrect specifications, and cost risks before you order components.

What is checked
  • Component availability & lifecycle (NRND / obsolete)
  • Single-source and supply chain risks
  • Incorrect or incomplete specifications
  • Voltage ratings and tolerance margins
  • Package vs footprint compatibility
  • Manufacturer part number consistency
  • Alternative component suggestions
  • Cost optimisation opportunities
Example findings
Regulator obsolete
Critical

Selected LDO is marked NRND and has limited distributor stock, creating a high production risk.

Fix: Replace with pin-compatible alternative with active lifecycle (e.g. modern LDO series).
Capacitor voltage rating too low
Warning

10V rated capacitor used on a 9V rail. Insufficient derating reduces reliability.

Fix: Use ≥16V rated capacitor to ensure safe operating margin.
